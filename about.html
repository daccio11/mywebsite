Welcome to My About Page

Hi, I'm Benjamin Daccione. I am currently a student at Marshall University, pursuing a degree in Cyber Security. My interest in Cyber Security started when I realized the critical importance of protecting information in our increasingly digital world.

Throughout my studies, I have gained knowledge in various areas such as network security, cryptography, and ethical hacking. This blog serves as a personal knowledge repository where I document what I've learned and share my insights with others. My goal is to create a resource that not only helps me review topics but also aids others in their learning journey.

When I'm not studying or working on Cyber Security projects, I enjoy exploring new technologies, participating in coding challenges, and contributing to open-source projects. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or if you'd like to discuss Cyber Security topics!
